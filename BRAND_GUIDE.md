
# TaiShing Brand UI Kit (Draft)

## Brand Colors
- Milk Tea Primary: #d7c4ad
- Cloud Beige: #f5efe7
- Rose Gold Accent: #c8a98b
- Ink Brown Text: #3a2f2a

## Typography
- Headings: Noto Serif TC
- Body: Noto Sans TC

## Logo Usage
- Use `logo-pro.png` centered on light backgrounds.
- Minimum clear space: 1× logo height.
- Do not distort, rotate, or change its color.

## Patterns
- Oriental Cloud Pattern (ultra-light)
- Used as background at 3–5% opacity.

## Components
- Navbar: Milk Tea primary with Ink Brown text.
- Buttons: Rose Gold accent with white text.
- Cards: White background, Milk Tea border.
